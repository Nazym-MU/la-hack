Entanglement study notes.

Two qubits can share a state that can't be written as a product of individual
states — e.g. the Bell state (|00> + |11>)/sqrt(2). Measure one, you instantly
know the other, no matter the distance. Not faster-than-light communication:
you can't control which outcome you get, so no information is sent.

For the exam: be able to derive the Bell states and explain why the CHSH
inequality is violated. Draw the circuit: Hadamard then CNOT.
